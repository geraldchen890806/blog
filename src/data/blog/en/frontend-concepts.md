## Zalgo, the Demon

A function that may invoke your callback either synchronously or asynchronously.

## Duck Typing

If it looks like a duck and quacks like a duck, it must be a duck.

## HTTP vs HTTPS

- HTTP: plain-text transmission, runs on top of TCP
- HTTPS: encrypted with SSL/TLS, adding encryption and authentication
- HTTP/2: multiplexing — all requests travel over a single TCP connection

## Handy CSS Tricks

1. `attr()` to pull values from `data-*` attributes
2. `position: sticky` for sticky positioning
3. `text-align-last` to justify the last line of text
4. `writing-mode` for vertical typography
5. `object-fit` to control how images fill their box
6. `transform` to draw 1px borders
7. `filter: grayscale(1)` for a grayscale "mourning mode"
8. `:valid` and `:invalid` for form validation
9. `:focus-within` to respond to focus bubbling up from descendants
