This privacy policy explains how chenguangliang.com ("this website") collects, uses, and protects your information. This website respects and protects the privacy of all visitors. Please read this policy carefully before using the site; continued use constitutes acceptance of the terms below.

Last updated: April 29, 2026

## Information Collection

### Automatically Collected Information

When you visit this website, server logs automatically record:

- Browser type and version
- Operating system and device type
- Visit time and duration
- Referrer URL
- IP address (used only for regional statistics, never linked to personal identity)
- Screen resolution (used to optimize responsive layouts)

This information is used solely to analyze traffic, improve the reading experience, and troubleshoot technical issues. It is never shared with third parties or used for any commercial purpose. Log data is automatically purged after roughly 90 days.

### Information You Provide

This website requires no account registration and does not actively collect personally identifying information such as your name, email address, or phone number. If you contact me through the channels below (GitHub, X), the information you provide is used only to respond to your inquiry.

### Cookies

This website uses cookies for:

- Saving your theme preference (dark / light mode)
- Remembering reading position and search history
- Maintaining session state for a smoother browsing experience

These cookies are stored only on your local device and contain no personally identifying information. You can clear cookies at any time in your browser settings, though doing so may affect some features.

## Google AdSense

This website uses Google AdSense to display ads that keep the site running. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

Google's use of the DoubleClick DART cookie enables it to serve ads based on your visits to this site and other sites on the internet. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting [Google Ads Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads), or opt out of some third-party vendors' use of cookies for personalized advertising at [aboutads.info](https://www.aboutads.info/choices/).

For details on how Google uses data, see the [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## Google Analytics

This website uses Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to analyze traffic. Google Analytics collects anonymous usage data via cookies (page views, time on page, bounce rate, device type, etc.) and does not collect personally identifying information. All data is IP-anonymized.

To learn how Google Analytics handles data, see [Google Analytics Data Privacy and Security](https://support.google.com/analytics/answer/6004245).

If you prefer not to be tracked by Google Analytics, you can install the [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).
